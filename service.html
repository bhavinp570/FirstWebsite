New business set up

Setting up a new business or a restructure in an existing business can be a cumbersome process. We feel that this step is the foundation of a business and it is essential to get the correct advice.

Our highly experienced accountants will analyse your situation and suggest the most tax effective business structure. These structures will also ensure that your personal assets are separated from your business assets and hence protected. Once everything is finalised the business can be set up within one working day.

    Our services include:

  • Incorporation of Company, Trust and Self-Managed Superannuation Fund (SMSF), Partnerships and Sole Traders.
  • Company Constitution.
  • SMSF trust deed and setting up bare trusts to enable SMSFs to buy property.
  • Incorporation of corporate Trustees.

    Registration of:

  • Australian Business Number (ABN) and Tax File Number (TFN).
  • Goods and Services Tax (GST), Fringe Benefits Tax (FBT), Fuel Tax Credits (FTC).
  • Pay as you go Tax (PAYG).
  • Workcover Registration.

Self-Managed Super Funds

Superannuation is often the primary vehicle for achieving retirement goals, tax planning and wealth creation.

Self-managed super funds (SMSF) have increased in popularity because of the control, tax benefits and flexibility for improved wealth creation they offer when compared to standard member retail super funds and member industry super funds.

Our highly experienced SMSF team has the expertise to help you develop your superannuation into a dynamic investment channel by simplifying the complexities, retirement planning, estate planning and the establishment of multiple income stream options.

    Our SMSF advisory services include:

  • Administration and audit services
  • Production of financial statements
  • Income returns
  • investment reports
  • Trustee minutes
  • Actuarial certificates and the fulfillment of all trustee duties and responsibilities.
